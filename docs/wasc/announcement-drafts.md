## 1. Press release

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Optio Academy Earns WASC Accreditation**

Optio Academy, the full-time online private school built on the Optio learning
platform, has been accredited by the Accrediting Commission for Schools, Western
Association of Schools and Colleges (ACS WASC).

WASC accreditation is one of the most widely recognized marks of quality in
education. It means the diploma and transcript a student earns through Optio
Academy are official, held to rigorous standards, and recognized by colleges,
universities, and employers nationwide.

Optio Academy is different by design. Instead of sitting through lectures,
students direct their own learning — pursuing real projects they care about,
documenting the work as they go, and having it reviewed by licensed teachers who
map it to high school standards. The result is a portfolio of genuine
accomplishment alongside an accredited transcript.

"Accreditation confirms what our families already knew: self-directed learning
can be just as rigorous — and far more engaging — than the traditional
classroom," said [SPOKESPERSON, TITLE].

Accredited transcripts are also available to partner schools that are not
themselves accredited, through a distance-learning arrangement with Optio Academy.

To learn more, visit www.optioeducation.com.

*Accrediting Commission for Schools, Western Association of Schools and Colleges ·
533 Airport Blvd., Suite 200, Burlingame, CA 94010 · www.acswasc.org*
