The first question almost everyone asks: does an Optio class really count?

Yes. When you finish a class, we send your local high school an official transcript. It's issued through Optio Academy, which is accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC), the same kind of accreditation your local public school has. The full details are in Transcripts and Accreditation.
